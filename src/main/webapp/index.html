Pain point
In the Cryptocurrency market,
it is not easy for private investors to achieve their target rate of return.
Notwithstanding the fact that Bitcoin’s value has grown more than 700% over the past year,
private investors are placed in an ironic situation where the more they focus on making profits, the lower rate of return they end up with due to various individual, market, and environmental factors.
You are having a disadvantage of insufficient amount of time and investment know-how that you need to analyze the market and chart. Furthermore, the rapidly-changing market trend makes it difficult for you to make rational investment decisions.
Since there is no circuit break in place in the Cryptocurrency market, the market price tends to significantly fluctuate. Also, the trade can occur 24 hour-a-day and the absence of relevant regulations allows big capitals to have a great deal of impact on the market.
Investing through the chart analysis is an investment know-how more ideal for the stock market, and it is less likely to make an accurate prediction by solely relying on historical data.
Solution
We provide the following three-dimensional solution to maximize the value of your investment assets
AI investment system built around machine learning technology Rix adds the Cryptocurrency to its intrinsic asset portfolio shortly after ICO. AI investment machines invest these assets to continuously generate returns. Rix is controlled by four artificial intelligence layers including RNN and CNN, which automates all investment related decisions such as the percentage of exchange, amount, and timing. It is made up of numerous investment modules making profits independently from each other. Rix aims to achieve the rate of return between 720%, the annual rate of return of Bitcoin, and 6000%, the highest return ever achieved by a professional private investor. This 0.671%, when converted to the daily rate of return, may seem an unrealistic number to non-professional private investors at a glance but they may actually find it very conservatively targeted in light of the current Cryptocurrency market constantly moving upward and running 24 hour a day let alone the fact that it is not obstructed by upper or lower limit of the price movement.
ICD Designed for the Continuous Growth of Rix AI increases assets and Rix's self-developed ICD (Incentive Centered Design) distributes returns to participants. It is a Cryptocurrency platform built around the mechanism where the overall profitability of the community is guaranteed by every individual member’s activity of seeking his own profit. The concept behind ICD is a little difficult to explain. But, if I do so briefly, it is as follows: There is an interesting experiment whose test subjects include a cleaner covering an extensive area of the street, crows looking for easier ways to get foods, and a trash can releasing foods in exchange for trashes. In this experiment, the crows keep bringing trashes to get foods while the cleaner regularly fills up the trash can with foods that will be used as rewards for the crows. In this way, both sides end up benefiting from each other. Rix has been designed to ensure coin’s long-term survivability and profitability by providing a withdrawal service and thus minimizing the risk zone of the investment shortly after ICO.
Transparent Management by DAO Rix transparently manages and distributes assets through DAO. In addition, Rix's DAO makes important decisions only through every participant’s vote. This can allow you to tailor Rix to best serve your investment needs through Rix’s voting system. Accordingly, Rix’s sole purpose is to help non-professional private investors realize profits.
